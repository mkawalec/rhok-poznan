About the Hackathon

Random Hacks of Kindness Global Event is a series of hackathons taking place at the same time all around the world. Groups consisting of 1-5 people solve problems and strive to get amazing prizes (TBA). If you want to take part, sign to attend!

Food (pizza), energy drinks and water will be provided during the hackathon.

Where?

Hackathon will be at the ZOO Coworking, located at ul. Zwierzyniecka 20, Poznań, Poland
